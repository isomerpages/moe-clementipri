### KidZania GO! to school
To create excitement for our P5 Clementians who will get the opportunity to visit Kidzania as part of their post-PSLE programme, we brought Kidzania to school instead! KidZania Go! To School is an educational outreach programme that provide students with the opportunity to experience the some of the authentic role-play activities found in Kidzania Singapore!

This year, we had a Chiropractic Clinic, Bank, Digital Film Academy and a Bakery. This is one out-of-the-classroom activity that will definitely entertain and educate!
