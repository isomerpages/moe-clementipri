<p>It is a positive community corner for the pupils by the pupils. It was
officially launched on 14 September 2023 to provide support and care for
fellow pupils. Pupils head there for peer counselling, study help, wellness
activities like writing postcards or simply a safe space to relax and seek
help</p>
