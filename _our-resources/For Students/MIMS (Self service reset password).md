<p><strong><u>Note</u></strong>: Prior to using this ‘<strong><em>Forgot Password?</em></strong>’
feature, you should have already set up your MIMS Challenge Questions. <em><u>If you have not done so, please approach your teachers for help.</u></em>
</p>
<p>URL for self service reset password: <a href="https://mims.moe.gov.sg/sspr" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">https://mims.moe.gov.sg/sspr</a>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Slide9.jpg">
</div>
<p>Enter your <strong><em>Student</em> <em>iCON email address. </em></strong><em>Click ‘</em><strong><em>Search</em></strong>’.</p>
<p>You will be prompted to answer two security questions that you have set
earlier in order to perform a self-service password reset.</p>
<p>Enter your answers to the questions correctly and click ‘<strong><em>Check Answers</em></strong>’.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Slide10.jpg">
</div>
<p>You will then be prompted to change your password.</p>
<p>Enter and confirm your new password, following the password requirement
guidelines on the screen.</p>
<p>Click ‘<strong><em>Change Password</em></strong>’.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Slide11.jpg">
</div>
<p>Your password change will take some time to be completed.</p>
<p>Once password change is successful,</p>
<p></p>
<p>Click ‘<strong><em>Continue</em></strong>’.</p>
