<h3>Non-Giro Payment</h3>
<p>a) Cash/ Cheque paying students will receive their monthly bills on the
16th of each month to pay fees for the current month. Eg: For March fees,
they will receive the bill on 16th March and they will have to pay by 26th
March. Payment must be made at the General Office by 26th of each month.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>For January and February (2 months), students will receive 1 bill and
they have to make payment by 26th February.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For November and December (2 months), students will receive 1 bill and
they have to make payment by 26th November.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>b) <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/fees?pt=Other%20methods" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Non Giro Payment Method</a>
</p>
